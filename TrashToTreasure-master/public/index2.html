Trash to Treasure, making recycling Fun!!!

What we are doing:
This new webpage and mobile application is created to promise individuals and organization with a better,faster,safer,cleaner way to recycle used or unwanted goods in your local area.

Treash to treasure:
Trash to treasure is an application that simple does what it sounds like, connects one person’s trash, to a person who sees this trash as treasure,or as people may say today someone's trash is someone else's treasure.



Easy benefit:
Today most people find or trade recycled goods through sites and organizations like craigslist, let go, freecycle.org, or the salvation army ect. which are great organizations but lack on the vision that this application has to offer like free goods, user friendly interface, safer user interaction, recycling and last but not least a fun experience all in one .

Geting it now:
Users can either download the mobile application or go into our website in order to participate in our new form of recycling using real time location ,or location using zip code or city. Users also have the ability to give or receive unwanted goods ,within the set categories of Fashion , Accessories , Home and Garden appliances , electronics , books ,children toys ,cars ,motorcycles and more.give this some thought ,The average American throws away 82 pounds of clothes every single year, this fast fashion alone is a devastating toll on our planet.

About app:
In the application The search results will include pictures, description, condition, location of the recycled goods. Once the receiver finds the treasure of interest, they are directed to the user giving the item profile for the receiver could ask for more information about the item;also for the receiver could have more information on the giving user, like reviews of previous items recycled.

Benefot not noly yourself:
When using “Trash to treasure”, you will help the environment ,and help the users who takes advantage of The application for recycling .The idea of recycling goods helps the environment because it reduce the amount of greenhouse gasses produced in landfills all around the world . A large amount of the waste that ends up in landfills can be used for other things with far less damaging effects on the environment. The best way to reduce the amount of good waste going to the landfills where it can produce greenhouse gasses is to reduce, reuse, and recycle as much waste as possible.
